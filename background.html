Haitian Hope Humanitarian Effort Inc.

About Us

HHHE currently has nine volunteers dedicated to serving the country of Haiti.  They donate their time and money to ensure that Haiti's poor are not forgotten. HHHE was established in 2010 by two sisters, Weslyne Coleman and Guirlene Durosier. They started with the goal of alleviating the suffering of Haitian people and to share the love of Christ with them by providing resources for education, healthcare, and nutrition. We carry out this mission through community services, evangelistic efforts, and mission trips. Haitian Hope Humanitarian’s Board of Directors, along with volunteers and advocates will work to restore hope and dignity to the people of Haiti.
