About

Join our Efforts and Make an Impact. "We want to alleviate the suffering of Haitian people and share the love of Christ with them by providing resources for education, healthcare, and nutrition. We carry out this mission through community services, evangelistic efforts, and mission trips. Haitian Hope Humanitarian’s Board of Directors, along with volunteers and advocates will work to restore hope and dignity to the people of Haiti."

HHHE is a non-for-profit organization established with the goal of positively impacting the lives of those in Haiti by restoring hope and dignity and sharing the love of Christ with them. We provide resources for education, health care and businesses. HHHE does regular mission trips every other year, and does remote work.

Donate

Donations help us keep the work going in Haitian Health, Business and Education. Haitian Hope Humanitarian Effort is currently helping fund three building projects, and are seeking to fund a free mobile clinic in Port-au-prince for Haitian Citizens. Visit our Donate Page to help contribute to Haitian Hope. Additionally, to find other causes to donate to, and read about Haitian lives and our work check out our blog page.

Trips

Haitian Hope Humanitarian Effort makes a goal of going and serving in Haiti at least once every other year. To plan a successful trip, we go forward in faith, gathering much needed clothes, school supplies, and medical supplies, and monetary donations as well as recruit volunteers. Our trips are always a blessing for both the volunteers and the people we meet. To find out more about volunteering on a trip, visit our Trips Page.
